# P2: Public sharing links, portfolios, trending, comments, and clone-tweak

## Goal

Grow Prompt Square/gallery community features for sharing, discovery, and UGC activation.

## Requirements

- Add revocable read-only share links for images or portfolios.
- Add trending ranking with week/month windows.
- Add basic comments with permissions, deletion, pagination, and anti-abuse limits.
- Add Clone & Tweak attribution compatible with lineage.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Share links do not expose private keys or private images.
- [ ] Tokens are revocable or expirable and rate limited.
- [ ] Trending ranking is explainable and tested.
- [ ] Clone & Tweak records source attribution.

## Out of Scope

- Social graph.
- ML recommendation ranking.
- Subscription billing.
